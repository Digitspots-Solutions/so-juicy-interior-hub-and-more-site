Frequently Asked Questions

What is an insurance broker?

An insurance broker is a licensed professional who acts as your advocate in the insurance market. Unlike agents who represent specific insurance companies, brokers work for you—comparing policies from multiple insurers to find the best coverage at the most competitive rates.

Why should I use Blessed Gift Insurance Brokers?

We provide independent, unbiased advice, access to multiple insurance providers, expert claims support, personalized service, and ongoing policy management. Our expertise in pension administration also gives us unique insights into long-term financial planning.

What types of insurance do you offer?

We broker all classes of insurance including motor, property, life assurance, health, travel, marine, professional indemnity, group life, and specialized corporate insurance solutions.

How much do your services cost?

Our brokerage services are typically commission-based, meaning you pay no additional fees—we're compensated by insurance companies when policies are placed. Our priority is finding you the right coverage, not maximizing commission.

How quickly can I get insurance coverage?

For most policies, we can arrange coverage within 24-48 hours once we have your information and required documentation. Emergency coverage can often be arranged same-day.

Do you handle insurance claims?

Absolutely! We provide dedicated claims support, liaising with insurers on your behalf to ensure smooth, prompt settlement of legitimate claims.

Are you licensed?

Yes, Blessed Gift Insurance Brokers Limited is fully licensed and regulated by relevant Nigerian authorities to operate as an insurance brokerage firm.

Can you help with both personal and business insurance?

Yes, we serve individuals, families, small businesses, and large corporations across all sectors of the Nigerian economy.
