About Us

So-juicy Interior Hub and More is a leading manufacturer of premium interior finishings and furnishings based in the heart of Ibeju Lekki, Lagos. We specialize in creating beautiful, functional spaces through our comprehensive range of interior solutions. From custom furniture manufacturing to expert finishing services, we bring your vision to life with exceptional craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Our experienced team combines traditional techniques with modern design trends to deliver interiors that reflect your unique style and meet the highest quality standards. Whether you're furnishing a new home, renovating your office, or completing a commercial project, we provide end-to-end solutions that transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary environments.
